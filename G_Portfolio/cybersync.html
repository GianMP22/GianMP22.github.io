Brand Identity: CyberSync
Illustrator & InDesign

#GraphicDesign, #SchoolProject

I came up with this cyberpunk brand for a brand design class. The idea was: with the normalization of cybernetic implants looks are everything. But you don't want your newly installed matis blades to catch on your fashionable threads, so you need smart threads. New from the CyberSync collection.
