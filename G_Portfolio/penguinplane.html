Plushy Penguin Plane:

#Prototype, #SchoolProject


Project Description:

TMy idea here was to try to use wearable technology techniuqes in soft switches to make a kid-friendly toy plane with a working propeller thats soft enough to snuggle with.


Process:

I made simple circuit with a battery, motor, and a soft switch made from two layers of 2mm craft foam, with a hole in the middle, sandwiched between two pads of conductive fabric backed with some gaft tape for added rigidity.
